VANILLA KIPFERLS
200 g butter
250 g floor
80 g sugar
100 g ground almond
2 packages vanilla sugar
http://www.bakersbeans.ca/images/2014/02/vanilla-kipferl.jpg
* mix all good up
* just roll a small roles, take a part, and make a kipferl (see image above)
* 175 °C -> max 20 minutes in the oven
* if they get a little bit brown, they have to leave (ATTENTION)
* they have to cool down 5 minutes now.
* after that, add vanilla sugar and powdered sugar to the kipferls
